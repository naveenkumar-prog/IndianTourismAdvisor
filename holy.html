Meenakshi Amman Temple With 33,000 sculptures

" It is estimated to have more than 33,000 sculptures and fourteen gateways that have two golden sculptures. The most notable sculpture is one of Nataraja which is built on the silver alter. The gateway is found on the southern side of the sanctum and it has a height of 170 feet. The sculptures are the most popular attractions in the temple.

Murudeswarar Temple Second Largest Shiva Statue

It is known to be the second-highest statue of Sri Anantadrishti, another name for Lord Shiva, the huge structure can be seen from afar. It is 123 feet tall and was built in over two years.The sight of sunlight falling on the statue is one to behold as the statue has been strategically placed in a way that it sparkles when the morning light falls on it.
